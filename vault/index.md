# Knowledge Vault

> _Awaiting first ingestion._
>
> This is a fresh template. Once you've completed the setup playbook in `CLAUDE.md` and run your first ingestion + synthesis, this page is the place to write a custom homepage describing **your channel** — what it's about, who's in it, where to start.

## After your first deploy, this page should describe:

- What your Telegram channel is about
- 5-10 of the most-discussed concepts (link to them with `[[concepts/<slug>]]`)
- Your top contributors (link with `[[people/<handle>]]`)
- A one-paragraph "how it works" so first-time visitors understand the system

For inspiration, see the canonical example at `https://clawryderz.spirittree.dev` (gated, but the homepage layout is the model).

## Browse

- Use the **search bar** (top-left) for keyword search across everything
- Use the **left rail** to browse Concepts and People folders
- Use the **graph view** (right rail or fullscreen) to see how ideas + people connect
- Every blue citation deep-links to the original Telegram message
